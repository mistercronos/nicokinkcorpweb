NicoMatt Corp was founded by two young 6th grade students called Nicolas Kress, During this year, Nicolas Kress and Matthieu Choueiri ( a close friend of Nicolas Kress ) discovered HTML and were able to create a website using Cloud9 and their imagination. they used their website to present NicoKink's games made with python. Their first game was Guess the number ( Made by Nicolas Kress and a little bit Matthieu Choueiri. ) Afterwards, NicoMatt Corp published another game called Dragon Survivor.
